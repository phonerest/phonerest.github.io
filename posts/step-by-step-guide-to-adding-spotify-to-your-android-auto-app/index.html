But how do you do this? It’s easy! All you need is a few minutes of your time and this step-by-step guide to adding Spotify to your Android Auto App.

Finally, you’re ready to start streaming music through your car’s audio system. You can do this by tapping the ‘Play’ button on your phone and selecting the music source as Spotify. You’ll be able to access all your favorite playlists and songs as soon as you’ve selected them.
