Finally, the last step is to save the screenshot. Once you have taken a screenshot, you will see a preview of the screenshot at the bottom of the Edge Screenshot tool window. You can click the save button to save the screenshot to your laptop, or automatically share it via a sharing link.

Using the Edge Screenshot tool is a simple and easy way to take screenshots on Microsoft Edge laptops. With just a few steps, you can take screenshots with ease and even share them with others.
