Q1: How do I set up voicemail on my iPhone 13?

A2: Before setting up voicemail on your iPhone 13, it’s important to bear in mind that you’ll need an active cellular data or Wi-Fi connection. Voicemail messages cannot be stored if you don’t have a reliable data connection.

Q3: How do I access my voicemail on my iPhone 13?

A3: To access your voicemail on your iPhone 13, open the Phone app and select the Voicemail tab from the bottom right corner. Select Play to listen to voicemail messages.

Q4: How do I delete a voicemail message on my iPhone 13?

A4: To delete a voicemail message on your iPhone 13, open the Phone app and select the Voicemail tab. Select the message you want to delete, then tap the Delete button.

Q5: How do I change the voicemail greeting on my iPhone 13?

A5: To change the voicemail greeting on your iPhone 13, open the Phone app and select the Voicemail tab. Select Greeting, then select Record to begin recording your new greeting.
