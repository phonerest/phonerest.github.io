1. Configuring Incoming Mail Server Settings for Hotmail#

The incoming and outgoing server settings are essential for users to be able to send and receive emails from their Hotmail accounts. Fortunately, setting up a Hotmail account on an iPhone or iPad is an easy process. Here’s how to configure incoming and outgoing mail server settings for Hotmail on the iPhone or iPad.

First, open the ‘Settings’ app and choose ‘Accounts & Passwords’. Now, select ‘Add Account’ and choose ‘Outlook.com’. Here, type in your Hotmail email address and password. Now, the iPhone or iPad will automatically configure the incoming and outgoing server settings.

Users can also manually configure the incoming and outgoing server settings if they are not set up automatically. To do this, select IMAP at the top of the page, enter ‘imap-mail.outlook.com’ as the incoming server settings and ‘smtp-mail.outlook.com’ as the outgoing server settings.

Once the incoming and outgoing settings are configured, users can choose the services they wish to sync such as mail, contacts, calendar, and notes. After that, they can tap on the ‘Save’ button to complete the setup process. Now, they can start using Hotmail on their iPhones or iPads.

These are the steps needed to configure incoming and outgoing server settings for Hotmail on the iPhone or iPad. Follow these steps and you should be able to easily send and receive emails from your Hotmail account on your device.

2. Configuring Outgoing Mail Server Settings for Hotmail#

Configuring your incoming and outgoing mail server settings on your iPhone can be a hassle, especially if you use Hotmail. This guide will give you step-by-step instructions on how to correctly configure the settings to ensure you can easily send and receive e-mails. First, locate the native mail app on your iPhone and launch it. Next, select ‘Settings’ and access the ‘Mail, Contacts, Calendars’ option. Choose the ‘Add Account’ option and then opt for ‘Outlook.com’. Enter your Hotmail e-mail address and password when prompted. Now that you are logged in, you will be directed to the server settings page. Here, you need to enter ‘imap-mail.outlook.com’ in the ‘Host Name’ field and ‘smtp-mail.outlook.com’ for the ‘Outgoing Mail Server’. Click ‘Next’ and make sure that the ‘Use SSL’ and ‘Server Requires Authentication’ options are enabled. Enter your Hotmail e-mail password once again and select the ‘Save’ button. Now, you should be able to send and receive e-mails using your Hotmail account.

3. Setting up Your Hotmail Contacts on Your iPhone#

If you’re a Hotmail user and an iPhone owner, you may be wondering how to set up your Hotmail on your iPhone. Here are four easy steps to get you up and running quickly!

First, open the Settings app and tap on Mail, Contacts, Calendars. Then, tap on Add Account and click on Outlook.com. Enter your Hotmail email address and password and tap Next.

Second, toggle the Contacts option to ON. This will create a new Contacts folder in your iPhone’s Mail app and sync it to your Hotmail contacts.

Third, open the Mail app and tap the Contacts button, which will take you to the new Contacts folder. You should now see all of your Hotmail contacts in the folder.

Lastly, tap the cogwheel icon in the lower-left corner of the screen and choose ‘Default Account’. Select your Hotmail account and hit Done. Now Hotmail will be the default email account for any new emails you create.
