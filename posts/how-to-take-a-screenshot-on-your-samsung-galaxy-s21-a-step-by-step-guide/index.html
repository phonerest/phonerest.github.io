Q: How do I take a screenshot on my Samsung Galaxy S21? A: Taking a screenshot on a Samsung Galaxy S21 is easy. Just press and hold the Volume Down and Power buttons concurrently for a few seconds until the screenshot is taken.

Q: How do I view my screenshot? A: After taking a screenshot, a notification will appear at the top of the screen. Tap on this notification to view the screenshot in the Gallery app.

Q: How do I edit or share a screenshot? A: After opening the screenshot in the Gallery app, tap the edit icon to make changes to the screenshot. You can also share the screenshot directly from the Gallery app by tapping the share icon.

Q: How do I delete a screenshot? A: To delete a screenshot, locate the image in the Gallery app and tap the three-dot icon. Select “Delete” and tap “Yes” to confirm.

Q: How do I take a scrolling screenshot? A: To take a scrolling screenshot, pull down the notification bar and select the “Capture more” option. Now, the image will scroll down and capture more content. Tap on the “done” icon to save the image.
