2. Clearing Cache on Samsung Galaxy S8#

Clearing cache has become a necessary part of your device maintenance routine. It helps your device run smoother and faster, prevents sluggishness and crashes. Clearing the cache helps reduce the load on the Android system, ensuring you get a better performance. This article will explain how to clear cache on your Android Samsung Galaxy S8 device.

The first step is to go to your phone’s settings. From there, tap on the “Application Manager” and scroll down to “Storage.” Here, you will see an option that says “Cache.” Tap on it and you will be able to view the amount of cache that has been accumulated by your device.

Once you have identified the amount of cache that needs to be cleared, you can press on the “Clear Cache” button. This will delete the cached data from your phone, freeing up space and improving your device’s performance. It will also reset any settings that your device has stored.

Another way to clear the cache on your device is to manually delete the cached data from the app’s storage folder. To do this, open your File Manager, find the folder that contains the app’s data, and delete all of the files inside. This will delete all the cached data, but it will also delete any saved data that the app might have.
