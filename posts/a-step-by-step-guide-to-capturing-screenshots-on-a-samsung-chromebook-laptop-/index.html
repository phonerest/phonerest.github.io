1. How to Take a Screenshot on a Chromebook#

Taking a screenshot on a Samsung Chromebook laptop can be done easily with just a few short steps. For starters, grab the keyboard and locate the ‘Ctrl’, ‘Shift’ and ‘Window Switcher’ keys. From here, press ‘Ctrl’ and ‘Window Switcher’ at the same time. This will capture the whole screen. You’ll hear a camera shutter sound, which confirms the screenshot was captured. The screenshot will also be saved to the ‘Screenshots’ folder of your Chromebook.

If you’d like to capture a particular window or tab, press ‘Ctrl’, ‘Shift’ and ‘Window Switcher’ together first to capture the screen. Then, click on the window or tab you wish to capture. After that, press ‘Ctrl’, ‘Shift’ and ‘Window Switcher’ again and the screenshot will be captured. The same camera shutter sound and saving process applies here as well.

If you need to capture a particular portion of the screen, use the ‘Ctrl’, ‘Shift’ and ‘Window Switcher’ keys to capture the screen, then click and drag the mouse cursor to select the area you want to capture. After that, press ‘Ctrl’, ‘Shift’ and ‘Window Switcher’ again and the screenshot will be taken. The screenshot will be just of the region you selected.

Being able to take screenshots on a Samsung Chromebook laptop is great for capturing images that you’ll want to save or share. Try these steps and you’ll be taking perfect screenshots in no time!
