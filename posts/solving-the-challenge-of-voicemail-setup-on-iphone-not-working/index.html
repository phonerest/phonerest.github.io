Having an issue with setting up voicemail on your iPhone? Restarting your iPhone is a simple yet effective way to combat this challenge. To restart your device, first press and hold the power button located on the right side of your device until you see the Swipe to Power Off message displayed. If restarting your iPhone didn’t work, another effective solution is to reset your network settings. To do this, go to the Settings app on your iPhone and tap on General. Then, select Reset and hit Reset Network Settings. This process will remove all of your current network settings and return them to the default settings.

If your voicemail still isn’t working, try restarting your phone and then resetting your carrier settings. To do this, go to the Settings app and tap on General. Then, select Reset and hit Reset Network Settings. This process will reset your carrier settings, including your voicemail settings.
