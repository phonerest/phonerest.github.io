Q4. What are some tips to make sure that the exposure is correct while taking pictures with a Samsung Android phone? A4. Here are some tips to make sure that the exposure is correct while taking pictures with a Samsung Android phone:

  • Use the exposure compensation tool to control the amount of light reaching the lens.
  • Use the spot metering feature to get a better reading of the light within a particular area.
  • When the subject is backlit, reduce the exposure and increase the shadows.
  • When the subject is in a dark environment, increase the exposure and reduce the shadows.
  • Use the histogram feature to check the exposure and make adjustments if needed.
