Scanning for channels is incredibly simple. All it takes is a few taps. Begin by powering up the TV and press the Home button on the remote. Then, select Settings and press the OK key. The next step is to set up your Philips smart Android TV. This is just as easy as scanning for channels. Begin by pressing the Home button on the remote and then select Settings. Next, choose the Network option and press OK. You’ll find the Wi-Fi and Ethernet options. Select the one that connects best with your current device and then press OK. Now you’ll be asked to enter your Wi-Fi password, if necessary. After that, you’ll be ready to access your favorite shows, music, movies, and more!

Thanks to technology, setting up and scanning for channels on your Philips smart Android TV is a breeze. Now, you can start watching your favorite shows with just a few taps!
