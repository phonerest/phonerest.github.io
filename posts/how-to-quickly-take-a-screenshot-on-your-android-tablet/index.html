1. Learn the shortcut to take a screenshot on android tablet#

Taking screenshots on your Android tablet is a quick and easy process. Fortunately, most Android devices have the same basic shortcut to take a screenshot. To take a screenshot on an Android tablet, press and hold the Power button and Volume Down button at the same time for a couple seconds. You should hear a shutter sound and a notification will appear, prompting you to tap on the screenshot to view it. Your screenshot will then be saved to your device image gallery. Alternatively, you can also take a screenshot by pressing and holding the Power button and tapping on the Screenshot option.
