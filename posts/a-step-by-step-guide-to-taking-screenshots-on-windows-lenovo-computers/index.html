Do you ever want to capture on-screen images on your Windows Lenovo computer? Now you can with this step-by-step guide! Learn the simple way to capture screenshots of whatever is on your laptop display, and save it for later with this handy guide. Screenshots often come in handy when trying to explain something, like troubleshooting a computer issue or providing instructions for a task. Thankfully, taking screenshots on Windows Lenovo computers is a breeze. Following this step-by-step guide, you’ll be able to snap screenshots with ease, no matter what version of Windows you’re running.

To capture a screenshot on a Windows Lenovo computer, press the “Print Screen” (or “PrtScn”) key on your keyboard. This will copy the entire screen onto the clipboard. You can then paste the image into any image editing program like Microsoft Paint, or save the image to a file.

If you want to take a screenshot of just a window, or a portion of the screen, press Alt-PrtScn. This will take a screenshot of the active window or a selected area of the screen and save it to the clipboard. You can then paste or save it in the same way as mentioned above.

If you’re running Windows 10, things get even easier with the new Windows+PrtScn shortcut key, which captures the entire screen and automatically saves the image to your Pictures folder.

So now that you know the three different ways to take screenshots on Windows Lenovo computers, you can easily capture the images you need to convey. And with the ability to paste, save, or share the images directly with your friends, you’ll have no trouble getting your point across quickly and easily.
