After you’ve deleted the voice message, it will be removed from your messaging app permanently. To delete multiple voice messages, you can use the multi-select feature of your messaging app. To do this, tap and hold on the first message you want to delete, then drag your finger across additional messages to select them. Then, tap the “trash can” icon in the top right corner to delete all of the selected messages.

It’s easy to delete voice messages from your Android phone once you know how. By following the steps above, you can quickly and easily delete any voice messages you don’t need.
