1. Explain how to take screenshot on Samsung S21#

Taking screenshots on the Samsung S21 is easy and fun. All you need to do is press the volume down and power buttons at the same time, and the screen will be instantly captured. Then, you can view, share, or edit the screenshot in a few simple steps. Here’s how:

After you’ve taken the screenshot, it will appear as a white notification at the top of the screen. To open it, simply tap the image and you’ll be taken to a page where you can view, share, or edit the image. To share the screenshot, simply tap Share to open a menu that allows you to share the image via text, email, social media, and more. To edit the screenshot, tap Edit and you’ll be given a range of options to edit the image, such as cropping, resizing, adding text, and more.

When you’re finished editing the screenshot, you can save it to your device or a cloud storage service, such as Samsung Cloud. To save the image to your device, tap the Save button to save it to your photo library or another location of your choice. If you want to save it to Samsung Cloud, tap the Save to Samsung Cloud button and you can access the image from any device.

Finally, you can also export the screenshot to a variety of file formats, such as JPEG, PNG, and GIF. To export it, tap the Export button and select the format of the screenshot from the given options. After you’ve selected the format, you can save the image to the location of your choice.
