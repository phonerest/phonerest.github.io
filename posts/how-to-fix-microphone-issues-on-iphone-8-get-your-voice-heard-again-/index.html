Third, software compatibility issues may be preventing the microphone from working properly. If this is the case, you’ll need to update your iPhone to the latest iOS version.

Lastly, hardware issues can also cause microphone issues. If you’ve already tried all of the above steps without success, it’s possible that the microphone itself has become damaged. If this is the case, the only solution is to have the phone repaired or replaced.
