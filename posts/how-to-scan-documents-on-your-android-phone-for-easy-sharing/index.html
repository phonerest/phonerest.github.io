Before you scan any document, the most important things to do is to prepare the documents. Firstly, make sure the document is placed on a flat surface. 6. Capture and Save Your Scanned Document.#

Before attempting to scan a document, it is essential to ensure that the document is properly prepared for scanning. It is also important to ensure that the documents are laid out in an organized fashion that will help in the scanning process.

It is recommended to use a paper clip or rubber bands to keep multiple documents together, and to use a scanner-friendly folder or binder to prevent documents from slipping away or bending during the scanning process. Positioning the documents straight and evenly on the scanner’s glass will also help in achieving the best results.

Once the documents are arranged properly, take a few moments to examine each page to ensure that all of the relevant information is legible. Illuminating the document with a soft light source can also help to identify any areas that may need to be readjusted.

Finally, delete any unnecessary content from the document prior to scanning. This can include unwanted logos, stamps or other markings that could interfere with the finished scanned document. Taking the time to adequately prepare the documents for scanning will help to ensure the process goes smoothly.
