i. Setting Up the Headphones#

Connecting your Beats wireless headphones to your Android device is easy and straightforward. All you’ll need to do is turn on your headphones and make sure that Bluetooth is enabled on your device. To do that, first open your device’s Settings app, then select ‘Bluetooth’ and turn on the toggle switch to enable Bluetooth.

Next, hold down the power button on your Beats headphones until the LED light flashes. This means that your device is now discoverable and ready to connect to other devices. Go back to your Android device and in the ‘Available devices’ section select your Beats headphones.

Once selected, a confirmation will appear to ask you if you want to pair your Beats headphones with the Android device. Tap ‘Pair’ to connect the two devices. Your headphones will then be connected and will appear in the ‘Paired devices’ section of your Bluetooth settings.

That’s all there is to it. So now you’re all set to listen to your favourite music on your Beats wireless headphones with your Android device.
