2. Synchronizing Contacts across Devices#

Copying contacts from an iPhone 6 to an Android phone doesn’t have to be a difficult task. In this tutorial, you will learn the simple steps to move contacts from one device to another. To get started, you will need your old iPhone 6 and an Android phone. First, connect the iPhone 6 to your computer and launch iTunes. Once your device is connected, click on the “Contacts” tab and select all of the contacts you want to copy. Next, click the “Export vCard” button which will create a .vcf file. After that, disconnect the iPhone and connect your Android phone. Once connected, open the “Contacts” app and click the “Import” button. Select the .vcf file you created with iTunes and your contacts will immediately be transferred to the Android phone. Finally, make sure to delete the .vcf file from your computer to keep your contacts secure. With a few easy steps, you can easily copy contacts from an iPhone 6 to an Android phone.
