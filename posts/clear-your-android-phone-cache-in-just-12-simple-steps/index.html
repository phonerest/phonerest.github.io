3. Step-by-Step Guide to Clearing Android Cache#

Clearing the cache on your Android phone is an important step to ensure your device keeps running smoothly. Here is a step-by-step guide to clear your Android phone cache in just 12 simple steps.

  1. Open your phone’s Settings.
  2. Select Storage.
  3. Tap on Cached Data.
  4. Tap OK to the confirmation pop-up window.
  5. Swipe up and down on the phone’s home screen to restart it.
  6. Open Settings again.
  7. Tap on Application Manager.
  8. Swipe left to go to the All tab.
  9. Find and tap on the application you want to clear the cache of.
  10. Tap on Storage.
  11. Tap on Clear Cache.
  12. Tap OK on the confirmation pop-up window.

By following these steps, you can easily clear your Android phone cache and improve its performance. To keep your device running well, you should clear the cache regularly.
