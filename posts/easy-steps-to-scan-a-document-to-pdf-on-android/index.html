Q1: What is a PDF scan?

A1: A PDF scan is a digital version of a document, which captures the text, layout, images, or other elements from the original document. It is created by scanning the document to a computer or device- readable format, such as a JPEG or PDF.

Q2: What are the advantages of scanning a document to PDF?

A2: Scanning a document to PDF offers several advantages, including better organization, easier editing, and portability. The file can be easily shared, uploaded, and downloaded with no risk of data loss. It also allows for better formatting and higher quality images compared to non-PDF files.

A5: Yes, there are several tips to make your PDF scans look better. Make sure the document is properly aligned with the scanner. You can also adjust the brightness, contrast, and other settings in the scanner app. Additionally, you should use a flatbed scanner for best results.
