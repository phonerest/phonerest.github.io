Tired of carrying around two mobile devices? Transfer your favorite apps and pictures from your Android phone to your iPhone with these easy steps! As an Android user, I often found myself dreading the transfer of my apps and pictures to a new device especially when it’s an iPhone. Fortunately, I’ve come up with an easy, step-by-step guide on how to transfer apps and pictures from Android to iPhone.

The first step is to download Google Drive on both your Android phone and iPhone. This allows you to upload and store images and files you want to transfer. After doing so, open the Google Drive application on your Android phone and select the images or apps you want to transfer. When that’s done, they will be uploaded to Google Drive. Next open the Google Drive application on your iPhone and download the files you’ve just uploaded.

The next step is to transfer your files from Google Drive to iCloud. First open the Google Drive application on your iPhone and select the photos or apps you want to transfer. Then click the share button and select Mail from the list of options. After entering your email address, click send and you’re done.

Now open the iCloud application on your iPhone and select the Photos tab. Here you will be able to see all the photos and apps you’ve just uploaded. Finally, click the download button and you’re all set.

With these easy steps, transferring apps and pictures from Android to iPhone no longer has to be a dreaded process. So, don’t waste your time, follow these steps and make your transfer experience a breeze!
